About Me

I recently graduated with my Master's in Business Analytics from the University of Maryland, where I built my skills in data science, business strategy, and storytelling through data.

My career began at Tredence Inc., where I worked with global retail clients. I built and automated end-to-end data pipelines, contributed to large-scale customer analytics initiatives, and helped design solutions that made complex data more accessible for business teams. Collaborating across engineering and strategy taught me how well-designed data systems can drive smarter decisions.

During my time at UMD's Smith School of Business, I served as a Graduate Student Ambassador, mentoring incoming students and representing the MSBA program. My research experience at the University of Florida further strengthened my passion for data-driven social science, particularly in understanding human behavior through analytics.

Beyond work, I find joy in fiction and psychology, traveling, cooking for and hosting friends, and spending time in nature and with dogs (and cats when they allow it!)

At my core, I believe in staying curious, showing up with kindness, and building a life that balances ambition with joy.
